About

The Person Behind the Pen

P.S. Kirby is a Maryland-based MM and queer romance author writing stories about men who feel deeply, love fiercely, and find their way through the mess of it all.

By day, Patrick works in healthcare administration and is finishing his degree. By night (and every spare moment in between), he writes. His books tend toward emotional honesty — complex characters, real tension, and the kind of endings that feel earned.

When he's not writing, he's probably being judged by two dogs and a cat who have strong opinions about his schedule.
